About

Junle is 100% HUMAN who is trained in the Visual Arts. She has worked as a designer for various digital production and brand companies in New York, San Francisco, Shanghai and now Toronto. Her past clients include places like Kering, Tapestry, BMW, Nestlé, eBay, FOREO, and FEED/DEPT. Inspired by nature and its forms and color palettes, Junle loves to incorporate these organic elements into both her work and her hobbies. She is an avid aerialist and watercolor painter.
